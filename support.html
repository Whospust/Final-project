Frequently Asked Questions

Why is the tape playing slowly?
Please check your batteries. When the AA batteries are low, the motor speed may decrease. Replace them with fresh alkaline batteries.

Can I record with the TPS-L2?
No, the TPS-L2 is a playback-only device. It does not have a recording head.

What is the "Operation" light for?
This LED indicates that the unit is powered on and the battery level is sufficient.
