What Makes It Great?

The Sony Walkman TPS-L2 is the world's first low-cost portable stereo. It changes how you listen to music by making it a personal experience.

Key Features

  • Stereo Sound: High-quality stereo playback from standard cassette tapes.
  • Hotline Button: Press the orange button to lower the volume and activate a built-in microphone, allowing you to talk to friends without taking off your headphones.
  • Dual Jacks: Two headphone jacks (labeled Guy and Doll) so two people can listen at the same time.
  • Battery Power: Runs on two AA batteries for hours of playback.
